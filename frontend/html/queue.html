Smarter Clinic Queue Management

MEDIQUEUE helps South African clinics reduce overcrowding by allowing patients to book appointments, join virtual queues, and track their position in real time.

What is MEDIQUEUE?

MEDIQUEUE is a web-based appointment and queue management system designed for public health clinics. It improves patient flow, reduces waiting frustration, and gives real-time visibility into queue status.

What the System Does

  • Appointment booking and management
  • Real-time queue tracking
  • Clinic directory with filtering by province and district
  • Staff dashboard for managing patients
  • Notifications and reminders
  • Analytics and reporting tools

User Roles

  • Patients: Book appointments, join queues, track status
  • Clinic Staff: Manage queues and update patient status
  • Admins: Manage clinics, staff, and system data

Why This System Matters

Public clinics in South Africa often experience overcrowding and long waiting times. This system reduces uncertainty by giving patients clear information about their position in the queue and expected wait times.
